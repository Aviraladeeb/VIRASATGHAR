What is Virasat Ghar

Every family holds a story the world will never know.

India is a country of extraordinary stories — Partition survivors, first-generation city migrants, business dynasties built from nothing. Every day, an elder passes away and takes decades of irreplaceable memory with them.

Virasat Ghar exists to make sure that never happens to your family. We interview your elders, write their stories with literary craftsmanship, and build your family a private digital archive — accessible only to you, forever.
